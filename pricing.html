FAQ's
  • Can I change or cancel my subscription at any point?

    Yes, you may upgrade or adjust your subscription at any time!

    If you sign up with the monthly option, your account will be a month-to-month subscription and you may choose to cancel at any time.

    If you sign up with the annual option, your account will be an annual commitment. DoInbound's annual options are the most popular subscription type because of the price savings we offer in exchange for the annual commitment.

  • Will DoInbound replace my current project management tool?

    Yes, you can transfer any current project you're managing in other platforms into DoInbound. Client project, internal projects, or on-going projects, they all fit into DoInbound.

  • Will I have to pay for templates or other features once I become a DoInbound customer?

    Everyone who joins DoInbound gets instant access to a set of agency-proven, pre-built templates.

    We've refined these templates from years growing our own agency, interviewing the top minds in the agency space, and working with hundreds of agencies over the years.

    In addition to those templates, you also have the option to purchase additional templates as add-ons through our Marketplace. Some of our Premium Training courses, like Let's GamePlan!, come with additional templates that are added to your DoInbound portal.

  • Can I track time inside DoInbound?

    If you sign up on the Pro or Advanced packages, you'll have the option to track time on every task using our native time tracker. The beauty of bringing process and project management together is that you can set budgeted time to your task templates. Once deployed, this budget helps your team understand the expected time for task completion.

  • If I start on one package, can I upgrade later to another one? How about downgrading?

    You can upgrade to a higher-level package at any time from inside the platform. You'll just be charged the difference between your current plan and your new plan. You may also downgrade your membership at the conclusion of your billing period. Just contact DoInbound support before you are billed for renewal. Your renewal date will be displayed in your Billing & Subscription page.

  • What is the best time for my business to start using DoInbound?

    It is never too soon to start thinking about the processes that will deliver the services that you sell to your clients. If you're a young agency, there is no better time to establish your processes.

    As you grow, more and more things pull at your attention. The sooner you start with DoInbound, the faster you'll have an established collection of templates that power your client delivery.

  • What will DoInbound help me accomplish?

    It will help you put a framework in place to organize the way you work as an agency.

    To scale your agency, you need a process that can be repeated for each new client you sign. This process can't just live on a shelf. It needs to be an integral part of how your team works.

    DoInbound will help you outline your unique process and capture it using our Template Builder. You can then use our Campaigns feature to deploy your templates for your clients.

    This allows you to track your team through the entire process, from close to delight.

  • I see the user limits, who counts as a user?

    There are 3 types of users in DoInbound, with many customizations you can make to fine-tune your user roles: Agency Admins, Agency Users, and Client Users.

    All three of these user types count towards your user limit.

    Don't hesitate to reach out with any additional questions!
